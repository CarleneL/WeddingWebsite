Where to Stay

We have a room block with discounted rates at the historic Loews Hotel in the heart of downtown Philadelphia. This recently renovated, art deco hotel (and America’s first skyscraper!) was once home to the nation’s first savings bank, Philadelphia Savings Fund Society. We recommend staying downtown so you can walk the city and experience Philadelphia. Also, our after party will take place at the hotel. Glen Foerd is approximately 20-25 minutes drive from downtown Philadelphia. Transportation will be provided from Loews Hotel to Glen Foerd for the wedding on 6/17/17. For reservations call 888-575-6397 and reference the Mason/Denis-Rohr Wedding Block or reserve online


If you prefer a suburban hotel with free parking, the Radisson Hotel Philadelphia Northeast is also an option, approximately 15 minutes drive from the venue. Book online with promotional code MDWB. Be sure to book by May 18, 2017 to take advantage of the discounted rate!
